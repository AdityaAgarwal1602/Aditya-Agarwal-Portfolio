üçï Pizza Sales SQL Analysis Project

The Problem: How can a pizza business identify its best-selling products, understand customer ordering behavior, and optimize pricing and inventory strategies based on real sales data?

The Process: Executed a full SQL-based analytical workflow using PostgreSQL. Created relational tables for orders, pizzas, and pizza types, and imported multiple CSVs using the COPY command.
